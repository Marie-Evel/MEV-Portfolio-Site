My Story

I'm an actuary turned UX designer. It all makes sense now. Looking back at my actuarial career, I've tended to gravitate toward special projects involving various aspects of UX design: I built complex applications, created templates to help my peers be more efficient in their work, designed interactive training documentation, and crafted data visualizations that tell a compelling story.

In the summer of 2019, I took the plunge and enrolled in a UX Design certification course at CareerFoundry. I'm now ready to take on the UX design world by the horns!

I've always been a designer; it only took me two decades to realize it.

I enjoy every single facet of the design process, from research to execution. No problem is ever too complex or too messy. I like to jump in, understand what's at stake, and work relentlessly until I find a solution to make things simple, digestable, and streamlined. And pretty.
